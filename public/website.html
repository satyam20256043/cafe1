The Problem

before they even walk in

In today's world, customers decide whether to visit based on your online presence, response speed, and reviews — before they ever taste your coffee.

Slow Replies = Lost Customers

Customers who don't get a reply within 5 minutes will go to a competitor. Manual WhatsApp replies simply cannot keep up.

Weak Online Presence

Low Google ratings, few reviews, and poor social media mean customers choose other cafés when searching "best café near me."

🚶

No Repeat Customers

Without a loyalty system or retention strategy, 70% of first-time visitors never return — even if they loved the experience.

📅

Empty Tables on Weekdays

Most cafés are packed on weekends but struggle on weekdays. There's no system to fill those slow hours with targeted campaigns.

👥

Staff Doing Robot Work

Your staff spends hours answering "what's your timing?" and "do you have parking?" instead of serving customers at the counter.

📊

No Data, No Decisions

Without knowing peak hours, popular items, or loyal customers, owners make expensive decisions based on gut feeling alone.
